1. Abstract

Project M is an autonomous, decentralized economic experiment built on Ethereum. It eliminates the "human error" found in modern crypto projects: no developers, no roadmaps, no marketing budgets, and no liquidity pools to manage.

It is a self-contained economy. The smart contract acts as the automated market maker, treasury, and escrow. Participants join by interacting directly with the contract. As the community grows, the "Level" of the system rises, mathematically increasing the cost of entry and the value of the treasury for existing holders.

3. Core Mechanics

3.1 The Level System (Entry)

The contract utilizes a global variable called contractLevel. The price to mint 1.0 Token is determined by the current Level.

Unlike linear models, Project M uses a Decade Multiplier system. The price difficulty jumps significantly every 10 levels, creating periods of urgency.

3.2 Mining (Maintenance)

Project M introduces a "catch-up" mechanic called Mining. If you join at Level 10, and the game advances to Level 20, your token balance remains static, but your potential to own more of the network increases.

A player can send ETH to "Mine" the difference between their entry level and the current contract level. This prevents dilution and allows active participants to maintain their percentage share of the Total Supply.

3.3 The Exit (Burning)

Liquidity is not on Uniswap. Liquidity is inside the contract. At any moment, a player can execute burnAllSoap() or send 69 gwei to the contract to trigger a full exit.

When you burn, you destroy your tokens and remove your exact percentage share of the ETH treasury. A 4% Tax is applied on exit, which remains in the treasury to boost the value for remaining holders.

4. The Hive Protocol (Cooperative Income)

Unlike a traditional Ponzi structure (Zero-Sum), Project M includes a mechanic for Positive-Sum growth.

The injectRevenue() function allows anyone to deposit ETH into the treasury without minting new tokens. This allows the community to build external income sources (merch, tools, ad revenue) and inject the profits back into the contract.

The Math: If the Treasury grows from external revenue while the Total Supply stays the same, the "Burn Value" of every existing token increases instantly.
